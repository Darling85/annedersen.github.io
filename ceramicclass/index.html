CERAMIC WORKSHOPS SUMMER 2023

Sign up to 4 Workshops (8h)or 1 Workshop (2h)


Spend lazy summer days taking a relaxing ceramic handbuilding workshop!
Creatvity comes first in this workshops and we will focuse on creating
unique and personal pieces in a relaxed and fun atmosphere.
The goal of these workshops is to facilitate your ideas and support you in the process of making your very own pieces.
You can sign up to a try out workshop (2h) or sign up to 4 workshops (8h) and go more into the depth of this craft.
It includes all materials you need to finalize your pieces:(Clay, Engobe Paint,Tools), 2 x Kilns.
Glazing will be done by the studio for you!


✿ You will learn how to make your very own engobe paint (underglaze) and work with pigmented clay.

✿ The class will focus on learning 2 methods of handbuilding to excecute your ideas.

✿ Triming and preparing your pieces for the kiln.
